Virtual Desktop - WIRED connection FLAVIA LIBONATI 12/12/14 Mostra

Dear students

for the next recitation: please, remember to bring the ethernet cable (besides your laptop) to be able to access Virtual Desktop. Indeed, during the "business/working hours" Virtual Desktop is available only via wired connection. Please, use the following link to configure your laptop for the wired connection: http://www.wifi.polimi.it/wired/

Nevertheless, you can access Virtual desktop remotely (e.g. at home) via wifi connection during the following hours:

Monday-Friday: 8 pm - 12 midnight; Saturday, Sunday and public holidays: 8 am - 12 midnight

Best Regards,

Flavia Libonati
